Lynx-dev -- Lynx text browser development

 

About Lynx-dev
English (USA)

This list is used for discussion of the Lynx text browser. Users can subscribe to this list; postings by non-subscribers are moderated.

To see the collection of prior postings to the list, visit the Lynx-dev Archives.

Using Lynx-dev
To post a message to all the list members, send email to lynx-dev@nongnu.org.

You can subscribe to the list, or change your existing subscription, in the sections below.
